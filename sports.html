KABADDI TRAINING

Best Workout Routines for Kabaddi Athletes

Focus on cardiovascular fitness, agility, and strength training. Kabaddi requires bursts of intense activity, so building stamina is crucial. - Incorporate speed drills, agility exercises, and plyometric workouts into your training routine. - Work on your raiding and defending skills.


  1. Squats: Build leg strength for powerful lunges.


  2. Bench Press: Strengthen the upper body for tackles.


  3. Push-Ups: Enhance overall upper body strength.


  4. Pull-Ups: Improve grip strength and upper body endurance.

Disadvantages

  • Physical injuries (sprains, fractures, bruises).
  • Respiratory issues due to breath-holding.
  • Skin abrasions, cuts, and burns.
  • Sprains and strains.
